## Account Management

Manage your Noor Al Shams account easily with these common procedures. You control access, security, and settings directly from the dashboard.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create Account" icon="user-plus" href="#">
    Sign up in under 2 minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reset Password" icon="key" href="#">
    Recover access securely.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I create a new account?">
    Visit the sign-up page and provide your email, name, and password. Verify your email to activate. Noor Al Shams supports SSO with Google and GitHub for faster onboarding.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if I forget my password?" default-open="true">
    Click "Forgot Password?" on the login screen. Enter your email, and follow the reset link sent within 60 seconds. Use a strong password with at least 12 characters.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I update my billing information?">
    Go to Account Settings > Billing. Add your payment method and save. You receive email confirmations for all changes.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in Security settings to protect your account from unauthorized access.
</Callout>

## Content Collaboration

Collaborate seamlessly with your team on documentation projects. Invite members, assign roles, and track changes in real time.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Invite Team Members" icon="users">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Project" icon="folder">
        Open your documentation project from the dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Collaborators" icon="user-plus">
        Click "Share" > "Invite Member". Enter emails and select roles: Admin, Editor, or Viewer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm Invites" icon="check-circle">
        Collaborators accept via email. They gain instant access.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manage Permissions" icon="shield">
    Use role-based access:

    | Role   | Edit Pages | Delete Content | Manage Team |
    |--------|------------|----------------|-------------|
    | Admin  | Yes       | Yes            | Yes        |
    | Editor | Yes       | No             | No         |
    | Viewer | No        | No             | No         |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Exporting Documentation

Export your docs to various formats for sharing or archiving. Follow these steps for smooth exports.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Export" icon="download">
    Open your project and click "Export" in the top menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Format" icon="file">
    Pick PDF, HTML, or Markdown ZIP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize" icon="settings">
    Set options like theme or watermarks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download" icon="arrow-down-circle">
    Generate and download the file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Embed Code Example" icon="code">
    <CodeGroup tabs="HTML,React">
      ```html
      <iframe src="https://nooralshams.app/project/abc123/embed" width="100%" height="600"></iframe>
      ```
      ```jsx
      import { Embed } from '@nooralshams/embed';

      <Embed projectId="abc123" height={600} />
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="info">
  Exports update automatically with your latest changes. For large projects (`>100` pages), use the API for bulk operations.
</Callout>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Export Issues">
  If exports fail, check file size limits (`<50MB`) and internet connection. Clear cache or contact support@nooralshams.app.
</Expandable>
